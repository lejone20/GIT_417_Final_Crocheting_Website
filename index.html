How it all started

The love of a grandmother

I have been crocheting since I was about 10 years old. My grandmother taught several of the grandchildren how to crochet the infamous granny square blanket. My first blanket was made as part of a group of blankets given to the elderly at a local nursing home. Our church's ladies group meet regularly to crochet, worship and socialize. My first custom creation was given to my high school best friend when I was 17, it was a custom blanket with our high school's name.

Creating a legacy

While pregnant with my youngest daughter, I began crocheting for a friend's photography business and decided to begin offering products for sale on etsy and facebook. Unfortunately, our little one had difficulty growing and I was bedridden for the last 4 weeks and our precious baby was born early a mere 1 lb 15 oz and 11 inches long. After a long 91 days in NICU we brought home our tiny bundle of joy who was still only 3lbs 10 oz and I began my journey of crocheting clothing for micro-preemies.
